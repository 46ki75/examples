# Kestrel Station

Kestrel Station is a fictional research outpost located on the Brunt Ice Shelf
in Antarctica. It is operated by the (equally fictional) Meridian Polar
Institute and has been continuously staffed since 2019.

## Facilities

The station houses up to 24 researchers in winter and 60 in summer. Its main
laboratory focuses on ice-core analysis, and it operates a small fleet of
autonomous gliders for under-ice ocean surveys.

## Power

Kestrel Station is notable for running almost entirely on renewable energy:
a 300 kW wind array and a seasonal solar field cover roughly 92% of its
annual demand, with diesel generators kept only as cold-weather backup.
